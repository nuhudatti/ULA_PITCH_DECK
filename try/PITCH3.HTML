5IRE Universal Learning Archive (ULA)

Built during NITDA/NCAIR SIWES - A Legacy Beyond Borders

Vision

To create a globally admired public learning infrastructure powered by AI where every student, intern, and professional can learn, upload knowledge, generate intelligent questions, and receive instant feedback — in a way that inspires nations.

Problem

Nigeria lacks a centralized, intelligent archive where students and interns can reflect on what they’ve studied, test their understanding, and build tech legacies.

Our Solution

  • Document Upload + AI Understanding
  • Instant Q&A Generation
  • Quiz & Certificate Generator (Python, AI, Cybersecurity...)
  • Real-time Feedback Engine
  • Intern Stories, Use-Cases, and Recorded Interviews
  • Export as Portfolio / LinkedIn-ready Achievements

Proof of Impact

  • Interns and corps members interviewed
  • Python beginner to advanced curriculum implemented
  • Certificates of real-world value issued
  • Students building real impact projects from the archive

Global Standards

This isn’t just local. 5IRE ULA follows global ed-tech models like Coursera, GitHub Archive, and Google Learn but with an African essence and AI integration at the core.
