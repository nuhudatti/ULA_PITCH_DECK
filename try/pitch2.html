5IRE Universal Learning Archive (ULA)

A Legacy Project by Nuhu Muhammad Datti | SIWES 2025

1. Introduction

Developed during SIWES at IBBU, 5IRE ULA is an intelligent archive to preserve and promote Nigeria’s tech education journey. It captures beginner to advanced Python, offers certifications, and includes real-world projects and intern interviews.

2. Problem

  • No centralized learning system for tech interns.
  • Knowledge from SIWES often lost or undocumented.
  • Limited continuity or national reuse of SIWES learning.

3. Vision

To establish a national archive that uses AI to document learning, auto-generate quizzes, provide feedback, and serve as a legacy platform for Nigeria’s digital future.

4. Features

  • Python Beginner to Advanced Curriculum
  • Certificate Generator for Real-World Projects
  • Auto-Graded Quizzes
  • Interview Snippets from Interns and Corps Members
  • Downloadable Study Materials
  • Planned AI Q&A and Summary Tool

5. Technology Stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Tailwind), Node.js, MySQL, GPT-4, JWT Auth

6. Impact

  • 25+ learners certified during SIWES
  • Python taught through hands-on project building
  • Interns confirm usability and simplicity

7. Call to Action

We seek NITDA/NCAIR’s endorsement, resource support, and integration to scale this into Nigeria’s first open learning legacy platform.

8. Quote

“A Nation that preserves its learners' struggles builds a library for future victories.” — Nuhu Muhammad Datti
