5IRE ULA Platform

A Smart Learning Archive for National Impact – NITDA/NCAIR SIWES Legacy Project

1. Vision & Legacy

The 5IRE ULA is an AI-powered Universal Learning Archive designed to make learning resources accessible, interactive, and measurable. A digital legacy built to scale nationally and echo globally.

2. What Makes It Different

Unlike traditional archives, 5IRE ULA can generate instant Q&A, summaries, and learning feedback from uploaded PDFs using real-time AI inference.

3. Real-World Impact

Interns and Corp members have used the platform to learn Python from beginner to advanced, complete capstone projects, and gain certifications.
